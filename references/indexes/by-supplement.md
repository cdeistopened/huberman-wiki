# Supplement Index

Every supplement discussed by Huberman with dosage, timing, form, and purpose. Organized alphabetically for quick lookup.

| Supplement | Dosage | Form | Timing | Primary Purpose | Caveats | Framework Article |
|-----------|--------|------|--------|-----------------|---------|-------------------|
| Alpha-GPC | 300-600mg | Capsule | 30 min before focus work | Acetylcholine (focus) | TMAO concern — consider garlic 600mg | `alpha-gpc-focus-protocol` |
| Apigenin | 50mg | Capsule (chamomile-derived) | 30-60 min before sleep | Sleep (GABA-A agonist) | Part of sleep stack | `sleep-supplement-stack` |
| Ashwagandha | 300-600mg | KSM-66 or Sensoril extract | Evening | Cortisol reduction | Cycle 2wk on/2wk off. Thyroid concern with chronic use. | `ashwagandha-nuanced-view` |
| Beta-alanine | 3.2g/day | Powder | Split doses (reduces tingling) | Endurance, buffering | Tingling (paresthesia) is normal and harmless | — |
| Boron | 2-6mg | Capsule | Morning | Free testosterone support | Low risk | `tongkat-ali-fadogia-protocol` |
| Caffeine | 1-3mg/kg body weight | Coffee, tea, or pill | 90-120 min after waking | Alertness, focus, performance | Cutoff 8-10hr before bed | `caffeine-adenosine-protocol` |
| Creatine monohydrate | 5g/day | Monohydrate powder | Any time, with or without food | Strength, cognitive, neuroprotective | Most studied supplement. No loading needed. | `creatine-brain-body` |
| Fadogia agrestis | 425-600mg | Standardized extract | Morning | Testosterone support | Cycle 8-12wk on, 2-4wk off. Potential toxicity at high doses. Blood work. | `tongkat-ali-fadogia-protocol` |
| Glycine | 2g | Powder or capsule | 30-60 min before sleep | Sleep (lowers core body temp) | Can combine with magnesium | `sleep-supplement-stack` |
| Inositol | 900mg | Myo-inositol | Before bed or upon night waking | Night waking, anxiety | Specific for middle-of-night waking | `sleep-supplement-stack` |
| L-theanine | 100-400mg | Capsule | 30-60 min before sleep OR with caffeine for calm focus | Relaxation, sleep | Some people get vivid dreams or sleepwalking. Start at 100mg. | `sleep-supplement-stack` |
| L-tyrosine | 500mg | Capsule | 30 min before high-demand focus | Dopamine precursor | Not for daily use — high-demand days only | `alpha-gpc-focus-protocol` |
| Magnesium bisglycinate | 200mg | Capsule or powder | Before bed | Sleep, relaxation | Alternative to threonate for sleep | `magnesium-forms-guide` |
| Magnesium citrate | Variable | Capsule or powder | Any time | GI motility, general Mg | More GI effect (can cause loose stools) | `magnesium-forms-guide` |
| Magnesium threonate | 145mg | Magtein / L-threonate | 30-60 min before sleep | Sleep + cognitive (crosses BBB) | ~5% get GI distress — discontinue if so | `magnesium-forms-guide` |

| Rhodiola rosea | 100-200mg | Standardized extract | Before stressful events | Acute cortisol modulation | Not for daily long-term use | `ashwagandha-nuanced-view` |
| Tongkat ali | 400mg | Standardized extract (10:1) | Morning | Testosterone support | Huberman takes this personally | `tongkat-ali-fadogia-protocol` |
| Vitamin D3 | 1000-5000 IU/day | D3 (NOT D2) | Morning, with fat-containing food | Immune, mood, bone health | Many people are deficient. Test levels. | — |
| Zinc | 15-30mg | Zinc picolinate or citrate | Evening with food | Immune, testosterone | Don't take on empty stomach (nausea). Don't combine with copper without balancing. | — |
