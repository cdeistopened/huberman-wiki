## Sleep & Circadian Protocols

| Protocol | Specifics | Framework Article |
|----------|----------|-------------------|
| Morning Sunlight | 5 min bright, 10 min cloudy, 20-30 min overcast. Within 30-60 min of waking. No sunglasses. | `morning-sunlight-protocol` |
| Evening Sunset Viewing | 5-30 min at low solar angle. Partially offsets nighttime light damage. | `evening-sunset-viewing` |
| Evening Light Restriction | Dim lights after sunset. Low-placed lamps, no overhead. Avoid bright light 10pm-4am. | `evening-light-restriction` |
| Sleep Temperature | Room at 65-68F (18-20C). Hot shower 1-2hr before bed drops core temp. | `temperature-regulation-sleep` |
| Sleep Supplement Stack | Mg threonate 145mg + apigenin 50mg + theanine 100-400mg. 30-60 min before bed. | `sleep-supplement-stack` |
| Caffeine Timing | Delay 90-120 min after waking. Cutoff 8-10hr before bed. | `caffeine-adenosine-protocol` |
| NSDR / Yoga Nidra | 10-20 min guided protocol. Restores dopamine. Use for sleep onset, night waking, or daytime recovery. | `nsdr-yoga-nidra-protocol` |
| Napping | 20-30 min max before 3pm. NSDR is preferred alternative. | `napping-protocol` |
| Jet Lag Protocol | Temperature minimum method. Light at destination time. 0.5-1mg melatonin short-term. | `jet-lag-protocol` |
| Inositol for Night Waking | 900mg myo-inositol before bed or upon waking. | `sleep-supplement-stack` |

## Exercise & Performance Protocols

| Protocol | Specifics | Framework Article |
|----------|----------|-------------------|
| Zone 2 Cardio | 150-200 min/week. Nasal breathing. Can maintain conversation but not easily. HR ~60-70% max. | `zone-2-cardio-protocol` |
| VO2 Max Intervals | 4-6 x 3-4 min at max sustainable effort. 3-4 min rest. 1x/week. | `vo2-max-protocol` |
| Resistance Training (Strength) | 3-5 sets x 3-5 reps. >80% 1RM. 2-5 min rest. | `resistance-training-hypertrophy` |
| Resistance Training (Hypertrophy) | 3-4 sets x 8-15 reps. Moderate weight. 60-90 sec rest. 15-20 hard sets/muscle/week. | `resistance-training-hypertrophy` |
| Cold After Training Caveat | NO cold within 4hr after hypertrophy/strength training. OK before or on other days. | `cold-after-training-caveat` |
| Sauna Protocol | 3-4x/week, 20 min at 80-100C (176-212F). Growth hormone 2-16x. | `sauna-growth-hormone-protocol` |
| Flexibility | Static holds 30 sec minimum, 2-4 sets/muscle. Separate from resistance by 6+ hours. | `flexibility-stretching-protocol` |

## Stress & Mental Health Protocols

| Protocol | Specifics | Framework Article |
|----------|----------|-------------------|
| Physiological Sigh | Double inhale nose, long exhale mouth. 1-3 reps. Fastest stress reduction tool known. | `physiological-sigh-protocol` |
| Cyclic Sighing (5 min) | Repeat physiological sigh for 5 min. More effective than 5 min meditation (Huberman lab study). | `breathing-protocols-comparison` |
| Panoramic Vision | Soften gaze to full visual field. Walk with optic flow. 5-10 min reduces cortisol. | `panoramic-vision-protocol` |
| Cold Exposure (Stress) | 1-5 min at 45-60F. Trains autonomic regulation. Deliberate calm under stress. | `cold-exposure-dopamine-protocol` |
| Box Breathing | 4 sec inhale, 4 sec hold, 4 sec exhale, 4 sec hold. 5-10 min. Pre-performance. | `breathing-protocols-comparison` |
| Self-Hypnosis | 10-15 min daily via Reveri app. Narrow deep focus + relaxation. Faster than meditation for neural change. | `self-hypnosis-spiegel-protocol` |
| 13-Min Meditation | Focus meditation 13 min/day for 8 weeks. Significant attention and stress reduction. | `meditation-types-comparison` |
| Gratitude Practice | Recall a story of genuine gratitude (giving or receiving). 1-3 min, 3x/week (not daily). | `gratitude-practice-protocol` |
| Pennebaker Journaling | 15-20 min writing about stressful event. 4 sessions over 2-4 weeks. Facts + emotions. | `journaling-pennebaker-protocol` |
